THE MECHANISM

The PepeEliza hook is inspired by Liquidum (0x33b604f...) — the first project to prove that a Uniswap V4 hook can generate its own liquidity autonomously. We studied it, respected it, and built further.

Every swap in the $PEPELIZA pool triggers the afterSwap hook. A 0.3% fee is collected from the ETH side of every trade. Never from your tokens. You always receive exactly what you swapped. The fee accumulates silently in the hook contract until it crosses the 0.5 ETH threshold. Then the next sell pulls the trigger.
